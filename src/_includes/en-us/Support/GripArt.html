Accessory Installation Guide - Grip Art

Content in the package

No. Item Quantity
1 Grip Art 1

Tools needed

  • None

Installation

  1. Clean the surface of the applying location.
  2. Remove the base paper.
  3. Put the Grip Art on the designated position, and use the arrow markings for alignment.
  4. Adjust it at the proper position, and press hard to secure.
  5. Wait 30 minutes after the installation before you ride again.
