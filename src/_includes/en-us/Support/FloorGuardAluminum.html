Accessory Installation Guide - Floor Guard Aluminum

Content in the package

No. Item Quantity
1 Aluminum Floor Guard 1
2 Rubber Washer - RF 1
3 Rubber Washer - LF 1
4 Rubber Washer - RR 1
5 Rubber Washer - LR 1
6 Hex Head Cap Screw M8*30 mm 4

Tools needed

  • 8 mm Allen Key

Installation

  1. Remove the covers with a plastic crowbar.
    a. Do not use sharp or metal objects as they might damage the surface of your Smartscooter™.
    b. If you intend to reinstall the covers, please note that every cover has a designated position: LF, RF, LR, RR. LF means left-front, and RR means right-rear.
  2. Place the Rubber Washers (2) (3) (4) (5) into the designated mounting pits. Please note that every Rubber Washer has a designated position: LF, RF, LR, RR. LF means left-front, and RR means right-rear.
  3. Put the Floor Guard (1) on the installation position and align it with the Rubber Washers (2) (3) (4) (5). Please note that the Floor Guard is directional.
  4. Affiix the Floor Guard (1) with 4 screws (2). The suggested torque is 19 - 25 Nm.
