Accessory Installation Guide - Helmet Strap

Content in the package

No. Item Quantity
  1 Helmet Strap   1

Tools needed

  • None

Installation

  1. Helmet Strap on the helmet hook in the trunk.
  2. D-ring of your helmet, fold the Helmet Strap and loop the other end on the helmet hook as well.
  3. Close the seat and your helmet can now hang on your Smartscooter™.
