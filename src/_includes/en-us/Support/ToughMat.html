Accessory Installation Guide - Tough Mat

Content in the package

No. Item Quantity
1 Tough Mat 1
1 Hex Head Cap Screw M8*2 mm 4
1 Screw Cover 4

Tools needed

  • Plastic Crowbar
  • 8 mm Allen Key

Installation

  1. Remove the covers with the plastic crowbar.
    a. Do not use sharp or metal objects as they might damage the surface of your Smartscooter™.
    b. If you intend to reinstall the covers, please note that every cover has a designated position: LF, RF, LR, RR. LF means left-front, and RR means right-rear.
  2. Place the tough mat on the foot position for installation. Please note that the tough mat is directional.
  3. Nm。Fix the tough mat (1) with 4 screws (2). The suggested torque is 19 - 25 Nm.
  4. Put the screw covers (3) over the screw openings, twist them clockwise to secure.
