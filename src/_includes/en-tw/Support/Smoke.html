Accessory Installation Guide - Visor - Smoke

Content in the package

No. Item Quantity
1 Visor - Smoke 1
2 Washers 2

Tools needed

  • 14 mm Open-end wrench

Installation

  1. Remove mirrors with the wrench.
  2. Put the visor at the front of the dashboard, and align it with the mirror screw pits.
  3. Place the washers in the screw pit of the visor.
  4. Re-install the mirrors. Suggested torque for the screws: 22 Nm.
