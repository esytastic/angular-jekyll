Content in the package

Tools needed

  • 6 mm Allen Key
  • 8 mm Allen Key

Installation

  1. Remove the hook assembly with a 6 mm Allen Key.
  2. Install the Y-Mount Bridge with 2 screw (3), the suggested torque is 6.5 - 8.5 Nm.
  3. Install the Y-Mount (1), the suggested torque is 19 - 25 Nm. Please note that the shorter screw (4) is at the lower position and the longer screw (5) is at the higher position.
