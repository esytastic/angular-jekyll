Gogoro staff used a common GPS speedometer to test the following items for collecting the data of speed, time and mileage under the test condition that fine and windless weather, the whole weight (including 1 professional rider and 2 Gogoro® Batteries) of Smartscooter™ totaling 175 kg or less, the tire pressure set to Gogoro factory setting and 2 Gogoro® Batteries fully charged:
‧ Max. Speed: Super Boost mode on, Sport Activation subscribed, ride took place on a zero degree slope asphalt road.
‧ Acceleration: Super Boost mode on, Sport Activation subscribed, ride took place on a zero degree slope asphalt road.
‧ Hill Climb Ability: Super Boost mode on, Sport Activation subscribed, ride took place on 10% (6 degree), 20% (11 degree) and 30% (17 degree) slope asphalt roads respectively.
‧ Max. Riding Range Per Battery Swap: ride took place on a zero degree slope asphalt road, at 40 km/h continuously, until the electricity fully ran out and power cut out.
