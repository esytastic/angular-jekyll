A Smarter Energy Network

The GoStation® battery swapping unit is easy to deploy, and with its low physical footprint, can easily adapt to a wide range of environments, making network scaleability and expansion fast, easy and more flexible.

Big Data Analytics and Management

The Gogoro® Energy Network compiles consumer energy consumption patterns from big data to predict and forecast swapping demands. This enables Gogoro to dynamically manage each GoStation® site, so recharge timing can avoid peak hours or as electricity demands surge. This results in more efficient energy distribution and reduces the burden on the energy infrastructure.

TAIPEI

GOGORO'S FIRST PILOT CITY

In August 2015, Gogoro launched Smartscooter™ two-wheeled EV and Gogoro® Energy Network in Greater Taipei, our first pilot city. During the peak expansion period, the energy network grew at a rate of one GoStation® site per day. The coverage now extended to 3 neighboring cities around Taipei, with more than 140 GoStation® sites established.

A Better,
Cleaner City

Approximately 36% of NOx and PM2.5 air pollutants in Taiwan come from vehicles. The Gogoro® Energy Network provides a smarter and more efficient energy solution that is a much cleaner alternative.
