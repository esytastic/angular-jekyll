Smart Charging

The new Gogoro® GoCharger™ battery charging hub can be used at home or retail locations and can charge a pair of batteries simultaneously. When batteries are placed in the GoCharger™ battery charging hub it connects wirelessly and can then send charging status and notifications to the consumer. Gogoro also utilizes the connectivity to manage charge levels, charge rates and the overall health of the Gogoro batteries.
