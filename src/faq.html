How Far Can I Go?

People think electric scooters are only good for short commutes, but Gogoro Smartscooter™ is different. A Smartscooter™ can drive up to 100 km at 40 km/h with two batteries in the scooter.

But like any vehicle, several factors will effect how far you can ride, including:

Riding Uphill - Climbing uphill consumes more energy and can reduce your range. It’s that simple. So if your commute has a lot of hills, plan on swapping power a bit more often.

Tire pressure - If the tire pressure is lower than our recommended levels, then the under-inflated tire will generate extra resistance which will drag you down. So keep your tires properly inflated.

Weight - The more weight on the scooter, the less distance you will go. So just keep that in mind when you bring friends along for the ride.

Wind - Even a strong headwind can slightly impact your range, despite us having one of the most aerodynamic scooter in the world. It’s only natural.

Your Riding Style - If you ride more aggressivley, it will consume more energy and reduce your range. If you prefer riding at high speeds or accelerate quickly, you’ll tend to experience shorter range with every battery swap.

How fast is it to swap batteries?

Way faster than you'd think.

Just find your nearest GoStation® using your Gogoro® App and pull up to the kiosk. Then pop your trunk, grab your two used batteries, place them in any empty slots in the GoStation®, and 2 new batteries will pop up - ready to swap right back into your Smartscooter™. You can be back on the road in less time than it took to read this.

We’ve seen people do it in 6 seconds. Think you can beat that time?

What's the difference between Gogoro and Gogoro Plus?

More Color Options


With Gogoro Plus there’s even more colors to choose from. Check out the new Yellow, Blue, Orange and Silver models. There’s nothing else like them. We just think you should have more options for how you want to stand out when you pull up to the line.

More Premium Features


1. Smoke Visor
Designed to seamlessly attach to the handlebar, the visor improves aerodynamics for a more comfortable ride while shading the dashboard to make it easier to read on brighter days. On top of that it just looks cool.

2. Brake Fluid Reservoir Viewing Light
We’ve tucked the fluid reservoir under the dashboard for a more streamlined look. But checking the fluid level is easy. We put a small window at the front edge of the dashboard, with its own LED built in. Your can check the fluid level at a glance, by putting a magnetic on top of the window. It's super clean, and super easy.

3. Adaptive Path Light
We always put safety first. To make it even easier to see at night we mount an extra pair of high luminance LED’s at the bottom of each side of the chassis. They light up the ground around you, helping you to see and be seen by other drivers and pedestrians alike. Cooler yet, you can customize the lighting patterns with our smartphone app.

4. Performance Shock +
Each of us rides with our own style. Some of us ride faster. Some sportier. And some of us just want more comfort. Whatever your style, greater control equals a better ride. With a simple adjustment you can easily tune the damper to best fit the way you ride.

5. Brake Light +
We designed our Horizon LED taillight to maximize visibility in traffic by making it easy to see from side-to-side as you move through traffic. And now we made your Smartscooter™ even more visible with the Brake Light+. By nearly doubling the brightness and increasing visibility from every angle, it helps other drivers be more aware of your every move.

Do I need a driver license?

Absolutely. With its powerful motor and high-performance build our Smartscooter™ is the equivelant of a 125cc gasoline scooter and is classified as a heavy duty motorcycle. So a driver’s license and white license plate is a must have.

Why charge when you can swap?

Our batteries aren’t just smarter, they’re easier, faster, more convenient, and way safer than old fashion battery chargers. No one wants to carry batteries up to their apartment or hunt for some charging station only to have to wait hours until they’re ready to ride again. And who wants to buy expensive batteries that are only going to wear out? Gogoro does away with all of that with always-fresh, ready-to-go power. It takes just 6 seconds to swap and go.

How does "All you can swap program" work?

Pick Your Plan - Choose between “Urban Subscription” for commuters and “Sport Activation” for better performance. With either plan you can swap batteries anytime at GoStations.

Find a Battery - The Gogoro® App quickly locates GoStations. Find batteries nearby or at a GoStation® near your destination. Reserve with one touch and they’re ready when you arrive.

Swap and Go - Swap and go in just 6 seconds. Easy. You and 10 of your friends could refresh your batteries faster than refueling a single gasoline scooter.

Ride On and On - The twin batteries provide enough go-juice for up to 100km of travel. When you’re ready just pop back up to a GoStation and in no time you’re back on the road.

All You Can Swap - It’s fast, easy and everywhere.
